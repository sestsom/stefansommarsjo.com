Untouched Slice of Greek Island Magic: Paralia Prasonisiou

On our trip to Rhodes in May 2025 we went to Paralia Prasonisiou which is tucked away at the southernmost tip of Rhodes, where the Aegean and Mediterranean seas collide. Paralia Prasonisiou, commonly known as Prasonisi, is a breathtaking beach that feels like the edge of the world. This unique double-sided beach, connected to the mainland by a sandy isthmus, is a haven for adventure seekers, nature lovers, and those craving an untouched slice of Greek island magic. ...
